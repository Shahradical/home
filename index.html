Lavender

Lavender flowers represent purity, silence, devotion, serenity, grace, and calmness.
Purple is the color of royalty and speaks of elegance, refinement, and luxury, too.
The color is also associated with the crown chakra, which is the energy center associated
with higher purpose and spiritual connectivity.

Gladiolus

Gladioli express strength of character, remembrance, faithfulness, and moral integrity.
The gladiolus, hence its name, is also meant to pierce the heart of the recipient,
conveying infatuation. Additional meaning can be conveyed through color choice,
using the standard symbolism of flower color from the Victorian era.

Lily

The Lily of the Valley symbolises absolute purity, youth, sincerity, and discretion.
It also symbolises happiness.This joyful symbolism means that it's very important to
give someone you love a bunch of lilies of the valley on the 1st of May.
